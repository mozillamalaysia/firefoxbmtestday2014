15 Years of Mozilla

The Mozilla project was launched on March 31, 1998 when the source code was opened up. Community was built-in from the start. The community grew organically, but we can no longer take that for granted.

Mozilla Malaysia Community

Being founded on July 2011 as small community in Kuala Lumpur. Hence, we are prioritising community building in the organisation. A core group across all functional areas has been formed.

What is Mozilla Localization?

The objective of Mozilla Localization (L10n) is to improve the world by culturally adapting Mozilla products by region and locale thus offering them to users throughout the world. By doing so, we create a world where the open web exists beyond linguistic, cultural, and geographical boundaries.

Firefox Bahasa Malaysia??

Projek Firefox Bahasa Malaysia is an initiative of local Mozilla Community or Mozilla Malaysia Community. Our aim is to enable non-english speakers to use Mozilla Firefox Web Browser for their daily use. Specifically, we are targeting Mozilla Products in Bahasa Malaysia. In the future, we also target to localize to other Mozilla Products such Firefox for Android, Firefox OS phone and more. We believed this initiative following our principal mission, is to promote openness, innovation & opportunity on the Web.
